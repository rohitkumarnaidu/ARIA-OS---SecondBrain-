## Disclosure Policy

1. **Report**: Send details of the vulnerability to security@secondbrain-os.com
2. **Acknowledgment**: Response within 24 hours
3. **Verification**: We verify the issue within 72 hours
4. **Fix**: Critical/High severity fixes deployed within 7 days
5. **Disclosure**: Coordinated disclosure after fix is deployed

## Scope

In-scope targets:
- `https://secondbrain-os.com`
- `https://api.secondbrain-os.com`
- Mobile application (Android/iOS)
- Public API endpoints under `/api/v1/`

Out-of-scope:
- Third-party services (Supabase, Vercel, Railway)
- Physical security attacks
- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks
