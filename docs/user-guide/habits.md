## Creating Habits

Habits are actions you want to perform regularly. To create one:

1. Go to **Habits** from the sidebar
2. Click **New Habit**
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Name | The habit name (e.g., "Read for 20 minutes") |
| Frequency | How often -- Daily, Weekly, or custom interval |
| Type | Build (start doing) or Quit (stop doing) |

> Start with 1-2 habits. Adding too many at once makes it harder to stick with them.

## Daily Check-In

Each day, open the **Habits** page to see your active habits. Click the check circle next to a habit to log it as complete for today.

ARIA automatically tracks:

- **Current Streak** -- Consecutive days you've completed the habit
- **Best Streak** -- Your all-time longest streak
- **Consistency Percentage** -- What fraction of eligible days you've completed

## Streak Tracking

Your streak increases by 1 for every consecutive day you log the habit. If you miss a day, the streak resets to 0.

> Consistency matters more than perfection. A 70% consistency rate over a month is excellent progress.

## Habit Calendar View

The **Habit Calendar** shows a month grid with colored dots for each day:

- **Green** -- Habit completed
- **Red** -- Habit missed
- **Gray** -- Not applicable (off day)

This makes it easy to spot patterns in your behavior.

## Missed Habit Recovery

If you miss a habit, don't worry. You can:

1. Log it late -- the habit history stays editable for the current week
2. Review the **Missed Habits** section to see what you've skipped
3. Get a nudge from ARIA -- the AI may suggest adjusting your habit frequency if you're missing too often

> The **Habit Nudge** feature (6 PM daily) checks your habit progress and sends helpful reminders if you haven't checked in yet.
