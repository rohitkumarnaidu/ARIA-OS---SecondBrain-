## Logging Sleep

To log your sleep:

1. Go to **Sleep** from the sidebar
2. Click **Log Sleep**
3. Enter the following:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Bedtime | What time you went to bed (24-hour format) |
| Wake Time | What time you woke up |
| Quality Rating | How well you slept (1 = restless, 5 = great) |

ARIA automatically calculates your sleep duration.

> Log sleep each morning for the most accurate tracking. You can also edit past entries if you forget.

## Sleep Score and Sleep Debt

**Sleep Score** (0-100) -- A combined measure of your sleep quality and duration. It's calculated from your logged hours and quality rating.

**Sleep Debt** -- The hours of sleep you owe yourself. It's the difference between 8 hours (recommended) and your actual sleep. For example, if you slept 6 hours, your sleep debt is 2 hours.

| Score Range | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 80-100 | Excellent sleep |
| 60-79 | Good sleep |
| 40-59 | Fair sleep |
| Below 40 | Poor sleep -- try to go to bed earlier |

## Wind-Down Routine

After 6 PM, the **Wind-Down** feature becomes available on the Sleep page. It provides:

- A suggested bedtime based on your sleep history
- A recommended wake time
- Relaxation activities (e.g., "Read a book for 15 minutes," "No screen time after 10 PM")
- Personalized recommendations to improve your sleep

> Wind-down is generated by ARIA's sleep AI agent. It adapts to your patterns over time.

## Sleep Insights and Trends

The Sleep page shows:

- **7-Day Trend** -- Your sleep scores plotted over the last week
- **Average Duration** -- Your mean sleep time across logged entries
- **Recent Entries** -- A scrollable list of your sleep logs

Reviewing trends helps you identify what affects your sleep quality, like late-night study sessions or early morning classes.
