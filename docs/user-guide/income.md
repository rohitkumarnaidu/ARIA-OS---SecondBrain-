## Introduction

The Income Tracker helps you log your earnings, calculate hourly rates, and monitor your income over time. Track freelance payments, part-time work, project revenue, and any other income sources.

## Logging Income

1. Go to **Income** from the sidebar
2. Click **New Entry**
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Description | What the income was for (required) |
| Amount | The amount earned |
| Category | Freelance, Part-time, Project, Gift, Investment, Other |
| Date | When the income was received |
| Hours Worked | Hours spent earning this amount |
| Source | Optional source name (client, company, platform) |

## Auto-Calculated Metrics

ARIA automatically calculates:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Hourly Rate | Amount divided by hours worked |
| Total Earned | Sum of all income entries |
| Monthly Total | Income for the current month |
| Category Breakdown | Distribution by category |
| Average Per Entry | Mean income value |

## Understanding Hourly Rate

The hourly rate is calculated as:
```
Hourly Rate = Amount / Hours Worked
```

Track hours accurately to get meaningful rate data. This helps you:
- Evaluate which projects pay best per hour
- Make informed decisions about taking new work
- Set minimum rate thresholds for future projects

## Managing Income

### Viewing and Analyzing

The Income page provides:
- **List view**: All income entries sorted by date with amount and category
- **Monthly summary**: Total earned, average per entry, best day
- **Category breakdown**: Pie chart showing income distribution by category
- **Hourly rate analysis**: Average, min, and max hourly rates
- **Trend chart**: Monthly earnings over time
- **Year-to-date total**: Cumulative earnings for the current year

### Editing and Deleting Entries

1. Click on any income entry to view or edit details
2. Update the amount, category, hours, or description
3. The hourly rate recalculates automatically when hours change
4. Delete entries that were logged in error

### Understanding Hourly Rate Analysis

Hourly rate is calculated only for entries where hours are entered:
```
Hourly Rate = Amount / Hours Worked
```

The analysis provides:
- **Overall average rate**: Mean across all entries with hours
- **Rate by category**: Which categories pay best per hour
- **Rate trend**: Is your hourly rate increasing over time?
- **Rate floor**: The minimum rate you should accept for future work

This data helps you:
- Decide which types of work to prioritize
- Set minimum rates for freelance and project work
- Evaluate whether your time is better spent on different activities
- Track your professional growth through increasing rates

### Category Management

Income categories help with analysis:

| Category | Examples | Use For |
|---|---|---|
| Freelance | Design, development, writing gigs | Per-project client work |
| Part-time | Regular wages from employment | Consistent hourly/salary income |
| Project | One-time project payments | Fixed-price deliverables |
| Gift | Monetary gifts | Personal monetary gifts |
| Investment | Dividends, interest, crypto | Passive income sources |
| Other | Anything that does not fit above | Miscellaneous earnings |

### Monthly and Yearly Tracking

The system provides:
- **Current month total**: Income for the current month
- **Previous month comparison**: Comparison with last month
- **Monthly average**: Average monthly income over the tracked period
- **Year-to-date**: Cumulative earnings for the year
- **Projected monthly**: Estimate based on current month's trajectory
- **Best month**: Highest earning month in your history

### Linking to Projects

To track which projects generate income:
1. Create a project in the Project Tracker
2. Log income entries and note the project in the description
3. Use category "Project" for project-specific revenue
4. Review the project's contribution to your total income

## Tips

- Log income promptly to keep totals and averages accurate
- Track hours worked even for non-hourly work to calculate effective rates
- Use categories to analyze which income streams are most valuable per hour
- Review income trends during the weekly review
- Link income to projects for full financial tracking
- Monitor your hourly rate trend to ensure you are valuing your time appropriately
- Set savings goals based on your tracked income data
