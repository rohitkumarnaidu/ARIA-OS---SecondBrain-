## What is ARIA OS?

ARIA OS is your personal productivity command center. It helps you manage tasks, build habits, track sleep, plan goals, and organize projects -- all with the help of an AI assistant that learns from you over time.

Think of it as your second brain: it remembers what you need to do, when you need to do it, and helps you stay on track.

## The Dashboard

When you log in, the **Dashboard** is your home base. It shows:

- **Pending Tasks** -- what needs your attention today
- **Active Habits** -- your current streaks and daily check-in status
- **Sleep Summary** -- last night's sleep score and duration
- **Daily Briefing** -- an AI-generated morning overview (available after 7 AM)
- **Quick Add** -- a bar at the top to quickly create tasks, log habits, or ask ARIA a question

## Create Your First Task

1. Click the **Add Task** button on the dashboard or top navigation
2. Enter a title (e.g., "Complete DSA assignment")
3. Set a priority (Low, Medium, High)
4. Optionally set a due date
5. Click **Save**

> Your task appears in the task list with a **pending** status. Click the checkbox to mark it complete.

## Set Up Your First Habit

1. Navigate to **Habits** from the sidebar
2. Click **New Habit**
3. Give it a name (e.g., "Code for 30 minutes")
4. Choose a frequency (Daily, Weekly, or custom)
5. Click **Create**

Each day, visit the Habits page and check off habits you completed. Your streak grows with every consecutive day.

## Log Your Sleep

1. Navigate to **Sleep** from the sidebar
2. Click **Log Sleep**
3. Enter your bedtime and wake time
4. Rate your sleep quality (1-5)
5. Click **Save**

ARIA automatically calculates your sleep score, duration, and sleep debt.

> After 6 PM, you can check the **Wind-Down** tab for an AI-generated bedtime routine based on your sleep history.

## Talk to the AI

Click the **Chat** icon or navigate to the chat page. Type your message and press Enter. ARIA can:

- Tell you about your pending tasks
- Suggest what to work on
- Analyze your habit consistency
- Answer questions about your goals
- Generate a plan for your day

> ARIA works even without internet by using local AI (Ollama). If the local model is unavailable, it falls back gracefully with keyword-based responses.

## What is the Daily Briefing?

Every morning at 7 AM, ARIA generates a **Daily Briefing** that includes:

- Today's pending tasks sorted by priority
- Your sleep score from last night
- Active habit streaks
- Any upcoming deadlines
- A motivational message tailored to your progress

You can view past briefings in the **Briefings** section.
