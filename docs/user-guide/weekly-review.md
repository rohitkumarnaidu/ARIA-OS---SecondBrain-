## Introduction

The Weekly Review is an AI-generated summary of your week, delivered every Sunday at 8 PM. It analyzes your activity across all modules and provides insights, trends, and suggestions for improvement. The review is generated by the Weekly Review agent (A10).

## What the Review Covers

The weekly review includes analysis across these areas:

| Area | What Is Analyzed |
|---|---|
| **Tasks** | Tasks completed, missed, patterns in productivity |
| **Habits** | Consistency percentage, streaks, missed habits |
| **Sleep** | Average sleep duration, quality score trends, sleep debt changes |
| **Courses** | Progress made, deadlines approaching, study consistency |
| **Goals** | Milestone progress, goals on track vs falling behind |
| **Time** | Hours tracked, deep work percentage, category breakdown |
| **Opportunities** | New opportunities discovered, applications sent |
| **Income** | Earnings summary, hourly rate trends |

## Viewing Your Review

1. Go to **Review** from the sidebar (historical reviews)
2. Your latest review also appears on the dashboard on Monday morning
3. Each section includes:
   - Key metric (e.g., tasks completed: 12)
   - Trend arrow (up, down, or flat compared to last week)
   - AI commentary with context
   - Improvement suggestion

## Review Structure

1. **Weekly Summary**: One-paragraph overview of your week
2. **Task Analysis**: Completion rate, missed pattern, productivity tips
3. **Habit Consistency**: Streaks maintained, broken, recovery tips
4. **Sleep Trends**: Average score, duration trend, improvement suggestions
5. **Course Progress**: Video completion, deadline warnings
6. **Goal Progress**: Milestone updates, revised timelines
7. **Time Analysis**: Deep work ratio, distractions identified
8. **Opportunities**: New matches, expiring deadlines
9. **Next Week Focus**: AI-suggested priority areas

## How the Review Is Generated

### Trigger

The weekly review is automatically triggered every **Sunday at 8 PM** by the Weekly Review agent (A10). It can also be triggered on demand from the **Automation** page or by asking ARIA in chat.

### Data Sources

The agent gathers data from all modules:

| Module | Data Collected |
|---|---|
| Tasks | Total created, completed, missed, overdue, completion rate |
| Habits | Active habits, check-in days, consistency %, streaks broken/maintained |
| Sleep | Average duration, average score, sleep debt change, quality trend |
| Courses | Videos completed, progress made, deadlines approaching |
| Goals | Milestones completed, progress changes, at-risk goals |
| Time Tracking | Total hours, deep work hours, category distribution |
| Opportunities | New matches, applications sent, expired deadlines |
| Income | Total earned, hourly rate, best-performing category |

### Generation Process

1. **Data collection**: Agent fetches the current week's data from all modules
2. **Context building**: Compares with previous week for trend analysis
3. **AI analysis**: Sends aggregated data to Ollama or Claude with the weekly review prompt
4. **Review assembly**: Generates an 8-section review with insights, trends, and suggestions
5. **Delivery**: Saves to the database and sends a push notification

## Review Structure Detailed

### 1. Weekly Summary

A one-paragraph overview that captures the essence of your week in natural language. Example: "This week you completed 12 out of 15 planned tasks, maintained a 4-day habit streak, and made significant progress on your Machine Learning course. Your sleep quality improved by 15% compared to last week."

### 2. Task Analysis

- **Completion rate**: Tasks completed vs. planned
- **Missed pattern**: Which days had the most missed tasks
- **Productivity tip**: Specific suggestion based on your patterns (e.g., "You tend to miss tasks on Wednesdays -- consider scheduling lighter work that day")

### 3. Habit Consistency

- **Consistency percentage**: Average across all active habits
- **Streaks**: Longest current streak, broken streaks
- **Recovery tip**: How to get back on track for missed habits

### 4. Sleep Trends

- **Average score**: Mean sleep quality score for the week
- **Duration trend**: Are you getting more or less sleep?
- **Debt change**: Is your sleep debt increasing or decreasing?
- **Suggestion**: Specific improvement based on your patterns

### 5. Course Progress

- **Videos completed**: Total for the week
- **Progress change**: Percentage increase across courses
- **Deadline warnings**: Courses approaching deadlines with low progress
- **Focus suggestion**: Which course to prioritize next week

### 6. Goal Progress

- **Milestones**: How many were completed this week
- **Progress**: Percentage change for active goals
- **Risk flags**: Goals falling behind schedule
- **Adjustment suggestions**: Revised timelines if needed

### 7. Time Analysis

- **Total tracked hours**: Including deep work and standard
- **Deep work ratio**: Percentage of time in focused work
- **Category distribution**: How time was spent across categories
- **Distraction patterns**: Identified time-wasting patterns

### 8. Opportunities

- **New matches**: Opportunities discovered this week
- **Expiring deadlines**: Opportunities with approaching deadlines
- **Application status**: How many applications were submitted

### 9. Next Week Focus

AI-suggested priority areas for the coming week, based on:
- Unfinished tasks from this week
- Approaching deadlines
- Goal milestones due next week
- Habit recovery plans
- Strategic priorities identified from patterns

## Accessing Historical Reviews

Past reviews are accessible from the **Review** page in the sidebar. Each review is stored with its week-start date for easy navigation. Use the date picker or list view to browse past reviews and compare week-over-week trends.

## On-Demand Generation

If you want a review outside the Sunday schedule:
1. Go to **Automation** from the sidebar
2. Click **Generate Weekly Review**
3. The agent runs immediately and delivers the result
4. You can also ask ARIA in chat: "Generate my weekly review"

## Tips

- Read your review every Monday morning to set the week's priorities with data-driven insights
- Act on the AI suggestions -- they are based on your actual behavioral data, not generic advice
- Use the trends to identify patterns (e.g., low productivity on Wednesdays, poor sleep on weekends)
- Archive reviews you want to keep for long-term trend tracking across months
- The more data you enter during the week, the more insightful and personalized the review
- Compare consecutive weeks to track improvement in specific areas
- Use the Next Week Focus section as a starting point for your weekly planning
